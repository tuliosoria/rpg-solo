## File Categories for Ending Logic

Files are classified into 15 categories that determine endings:

- **autopsy** — Autopsy reports and biological evidence
- **military** — Military operations and directives
- **witness** — Eyewitness testimony
- **neural** — Neural experiments and psychic data
- **surveillance** — Surveillance and monitoring logs
- **containment** — Containment protocols
- **crash** — UFO crash evidence
- **cover** — Cover-up operations
- **signal** — Signal analysis and communications
- **classified** — Highly classified documents
- **personal** — Personal accounts and journals
- **scientific** — Scientific analysis
- **conspiracy** — Conspiracy and disinformation files
- **audio** — Audio recordings and transcripts
- **visual** — Visual evidence and photographs

## Ending Details

### 1. HackerKid Caught
- **Trigger:** Detection reaches 100% OR wrong attempts reach 8 OR override lockout (3 failed attempts) OR Turing test failure
- **Not a dossier ending** — this is a game-over state
- **AOL Headline:** `BRAZILIAN TEENAGER ARRESTED FOR HACKING MILITARY SYSTEMS`
- **AOL Body:** Government announces arrest of minor who penetrated classified Air Force database. No classified material was compromised. Investigation ongoing.
- **Image:** `ending_hackerkid_caught.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** You got sloppy. The system caught you before you could do anything. You are a cautionary footnote.

### 2. Secret Ending
- **Trigger:** Saved files include ALL of: autopsy + military + witness + neural + surveillance + containment + crash + cover + signal + classified (10+ categories covered)
- **AOL Headline:** `THE TRUTH WAS NEVER MEANT TO BE FOUND`
- **AOL Body:** A complete archive of classified military intelligence has surfaced online. The documents span three decades of contact, containment, and cover-up. No government has issued a statement. UFO74 has gone silent.
- **Image:** `ending_secret.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** You found everything. Every file, every secret. UFO74 can rest. The truth is out. But the truth is too complete — too dangerous. This is the ending where you understand the full scope of the harvest.

### 3. UFO74 Exposed
- **Trigger:** Saved files include ghost_in_machine OR files tagged as UFO74-identity-revealing
- **AOL Headline:** `FUGITIVE INTELLIGENCE OFFICER IDENTIFIED IN DATA BREACH`
- **AOL Body:** Carlos Eduardo Ferreira, former Air Force intelligence analyst missing since 1997, has been identified as the source of a classified data leak. Authorities describe the breach as a national security threat.
- **Image:** `ending_ufo74_exposed.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** You saved UFO74's identity file. You exposed your mentor. The system can now find him. The truth cost someone their life.

### 4. Real Ending
- **Trigger:** Files span 3+ categories including autopsy AND military AND at least one of (witness, neural, crash)
- **AOL Headline:** `LEAKED MILITARY FILES SUGGEST NON-HUMAN BIOLOGICAL SPECIMENS RECOVERED IN BRAZIL`
- **AOL Body:** An anonymous source has released a collection of classified Brazilian Air Force documents describing the recovery and examination of non-human biological material near Varginha, Minas Gerais, in January 1996. Officials deny the claims.
- **Image:** `ending_real.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** The real story. Solid evidence across multiple categories. Enough to start a real investigation. This is the "journalist" ending — you built a credible case.

### 5. Deep State
- **Trigger:** Files heavily weighted toward military + surveillance + classified + cover
- **AOL Headline:** `LEAKED FILES REVEAL MASSIVE GOVERNMENT SURVEILLANCE PROGRAM`
- **AOL Body:** Documents leaked from Brazilian military servers describe a decades-long surveillance and containment operation. The scope and secrecy of the program have shocked intelligence analysts worldwide.
- **Image:** `ending_deep_state.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** You found the machine but not the alien. You exposed the cover-up infrastructure but not what it was covering up. The public sees a government scandal, not an alien truth.

### 6. Alien Autopsy
- **Trigger:** Files heavily weighted toward autopsy + scientific + neural
- **AOL Headline:** `GRAPHIC AUTOPSY IMAGES SURFACE ONLINE — AUTHORITIES DENY AUTHENTICITY`
- **AOL Body:** A series of medical and autopsy documents describing the examination of a non-human organism have been published online. Government officials call the documents fabricated. Independent forensic analysts have not reached consensus.
- **Image:** `ending_alien_autopsy.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** You focused on the biological evidence. The world sees the body but not the system that captured it. Debate rages over authenticity. The real horror — that it was studied, alive — is lost in the spectacle.

### 7. Whistleblower
- **Trigger:** Files heavily weighted toward personal + witness + military
- **AOL Headline:** `MILITARY WHISTLEBLOWER RELEASES PERSONAL ACCOUNTS OF UFO ENCOUNTER`
- **AOL Body:** Personal journals and witness accounts from Brazilian military personnel describe an encounter with an unknown entity in 1996. The emotional weight of the accounts has drawn significant public attention.
- **Image:** `ending_whistleblower.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** The human story. You saved the personal accounts — the fear, the doubt, the moral injury. The world responds to the emotion, not the data. It is the most sympathetic ending but the least conclusive.

### 8. Containment Breach
- **Trigger:** Files heavily weighted toward containment + neural + signal
- **AOL Headline:** `CLASSIFIED CONTAINMENT PROTOCOLS LEAKED — UNKNOWN SUBJECT STILL HELD`
- **AOL Body:** Documents describing the containment of an unidentified entity have been leaked from a military installation. The protocols suggest the subject remains in custody. Location unknown.
- **Image:** `ending_containment_breach.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** You exposed the prison. PRISONER_45 is still there, but now the world knows. The question shifts from "did it happen" to "where is it now." This ending has the most potential to change things — but the entity is still captive.

### 9. Mass Hysteria
- **Trigger:** Files heavily weighted toward witness + personal + conspiracy
- **AOL Headline:** `MASS HYSTERIA OR MASS COVER-UP? LEAKED FILES REIGNITE VARGINHA DEBATE`
- **AOL Body:** A collection of witness statements and conspiracy theories from the 1996 Varginha incident have surfaced. Skeptics call it mass hysteria. Believers call it vindication. The truth remains elusive.
- **Image:** `ending_mass_hysteria.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** You saved the noise, not the signal. Witness accounts without hard evidence. The world adds your leak to the existing conspiracy pile. Nothing changes. The truth drowns in opinion.

### 10. Men in Black
- **Trigger:** Files heavily weighted toward surveillance + cover + conspiracy
- **AOL Headline:** `WHO IS WATCHING THE WATCHERS? LEAKED SURVEILLANCE LOGS SPARK OUTRAGE`
- **AOL Body:** Surveillance logs from a classified Brazilian military operation reveal the systematic monitoring and intimidation of civilians. The logs suggest a coordinated effort to suppress information about an event in 1996.
- **Image:** `ending_men_in_black.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** You found the watchers but not what they were watching. The public fixates on government overreach. The alien truth is secondary to the abuse of power. A political scandal, not a cosmic revelation.

### 11. Cover Up
- **Trigger:** Files weighted toward cover + military + classified but lacking autopsy or witness
- **AOL Headline:** `CLASSIFIED DOCUMENTS HINT AT MAJOR COVER-UP — DETAILS REMAIN UNCLEAR`
- **AOL Body:** A small collection of classified documents from the Brazilian military has been leaked online. The documents suggest a significant cover-up operation but provide few specifics. Analysts say the evidence is incomplete.
- **Image:** `ending_cover_up.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** Not enough. You proved something is being hidden but not what. The world shrugs. Another government secret. The leak fades from the news cycle within a week.

### 12. Ridiculed (Fallback)
- **Trigger:** No other ending matches. Typically weak or unfocused file combinations.
- **AOL Headline:** `ANONYMOUS HACKER RELEASES "ALIEN FILES" — INTERNET RESPONDS WITH MEMES`
- **AOL Body:** An anonymous source claiming to have hacked Brazilian military servers has released a collection of documents they allege prove alien contact. The documents have been widely ridiculed online. No credible news organization has picked up the story.
- **Image:** `ending_ridiculed.webp`
- **Narrative meaning:** You failed to build a credible case. The truth is out there, but nobody believes you. Your leak becomes a joke. The system wins by default — not through suppression but through irrelevance.
