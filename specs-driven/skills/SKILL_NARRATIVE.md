# SKILL: Narrative

> Use when: writing or editing any in-game file, UFO74 dialogue, ending screen text, or tutorial content.

---

## Before You Write

1. Read [NARRATIVE.md](../NARRATIVE.md) — understand the full arc, the tone, the voice
2. Read [CHARACTERS.md](../CHARACTERS.md) — match the voice of whoever is "speaking" in the document
3. Know which act this content belongs to — early files feel bureaucratic, late files feel desperate

## Rules

### Tone
- Horror lives in what is implied, not stated
- Never explain the creature, the harvest, or the cycle directly
- Let the reader's imagination do the worst work
- Brazilian military formality cracking under pressure — that is the voice
- X-Files atmosphere: the truth is institutional, not supernatural

### File Content
- Every in-game file: 50–100 words. No exceptions.
- Every word earns its place. Cut anything decorative.
- Files are not exposition dumps — they are fragments of a larger picture
- The gap between what files say and what they imply is where the game lives
- Each file should make the player feel something specific: dread, pity, confusion, paranoia

### UFO74 Dialogue
- Lowercase always. Periods only. Short sentences.
- Trust level determines verbosity (see CHARACTERS.md)
- Never uses exclamation marks
- References files the player has read
- Gets more cryptic as detection rises

### The 2026 Detail
- 30-year cycle from 1996 = 2026
- Handle with implication, not exposition
- UFO74 knows. PRISONER_45 fears it. The files hint at it.
- The player should piece it together, not be told

### Endings
- AOL headlines: punchy, 1996 news style
- AOL body text: short paragraph, journalistic, period-appropriate
- Match tone to ending severity — Ridiculed is dismissive, Secret is ominous

## After You Write

1. Read the content aloud — does it sound like a 1990s military document?
2. Is it under 100 words? Cut until it is.
3. Does it imply more than it states? If not, rewrite.
4. Flag for translation — update [FILES_REGISTRY.md](../FILES_REGISTRY.md) translation status
5. Add UFO74 reaction text if applicable
